## 0.7.0
- Complete rebuild from first principles because why not!
- All three themes now ship with *Night* and *Day* variants
- Day: backgrounds lifted to matte daylight depth, same identity

- Semantic 5th layer: readonly, static, async, abstract, deprecated, defaultLibrary

- Full scope coverage: SQL, shell, Rust, Python, TypeScript, CSS, Markdown and more
